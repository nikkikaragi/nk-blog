My Favorite Summer Table Settings

12 June 2026  •  Lifestyle

Creating a beautiful outdoor dinner table doesn't have to cost a fortune. With a few simple touches — fresh flowers, layered linens, mismatched vintage glasses — you can set a scene that feels genuinely special.

Start with a white tablecloth as your base. It photographs beautifully and makes every other color pop. Layer a linen runner in a warm terracotta or dusty pink down the center, and you have instant warmth.

For flowers, I always raid whatever is blooming in the garden — or grab a mixed bunch from the grocery store. Trim them short and cluster them in small vessels dotted down the table rather than one big centerpiece. It creates a more intimate, lived-in feel.

Candles are non-negotiable. Even at a daytime brunch they add softness to every photo and make guests feel like the occasion was planned with love. Taper candles in mismatched candlesticks are my absolute favorite — thrift stores are goldmines for these.

Finally, fold your napkins simply and tuck a small sprig of rosemary or a single dried flower under the fold. It is the kind of detail no one expects and everyone notices. Happy hosting!
